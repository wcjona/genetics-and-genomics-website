Welcome to the Genetics and Genomics Club!

The purpose of the Genetics and Genomics club is to promote research and career based opportunities for students interested in the field. The club also aims to connect students to related career resources, such as information relating to biotechnology companies. All faculties and majors are welcomed to join- it is intended for those interested in the field of Genetics and thus is not limited to only students studying science.

About Us

We offer a series of events and opportunities for students to learn and connect with others in the genetics and genomics field. Speaker Series are hosted by professors who are involved with genetics research, guest speaker workshops from related biotechnology companies, first year tips sessions, and workshops on how to get the most out of your biology degree. Additionally, social events such as meet and greets are aimed at connecting like minded students with regards to the field of genetics.
